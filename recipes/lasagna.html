lasagna

Description

Here is a lovely recipe for lasagna

Ingredients

  • Pasta shells
  • Tomato paste
  • Cheese
  • Ground meat

Steps

  1. Pre heat overn to 400
  2. Mix meat and tomato paste
  3. Lay one lay over shells into baking dish
  4. Add mixture
  5. Add cheese
  6. repeat steps 3-5
